Welcome to the Guelph Food Guide!

The Guelph Food Guide (GFG) aims to promote education about nutritious, affordable, and available local food distribution resources on the University Campus for students and staff.

Nutrition Facts

The Nutrition Facts page gives insight onto what a healthy eating habit consists of. Inside, you can find facts about different daily values to be met for calories, as well as facts for the different food groups.
